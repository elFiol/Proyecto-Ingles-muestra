Bullying and cyberbullying are two forms of harmful behavior that can have a profound impact on people's lives. As technology has advanced, cyberbullying has become more prevalent, but traditional bullying in physical environments, such as schools, remains a significant issue. This report will provide an overview of both issues, including definitions, causes, consequences, and prevention measures.

how to prevent.

Preventing bullying and cyberbullying starts with education and awareness. In schools, it is essential to foster an inclusive and safe environment by promoting empathy and peaceful conflict resolution. Parents and guardians should also remain vigilant for signs of bullying and cyberbullying, maintaining open communication with their children to intervene early. Furthermore, it is crucial for online platforms and social networks to implement measures to prevent cyberbullying, such as blocking abusive users and promoting respectful online behavior.
